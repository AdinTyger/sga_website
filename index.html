What is SGA?

Student Government Association is an organization created to amplify the voice of the student body here at Texas A&M University Galveston Campus. We represent student groups from across the university, and provide support for other student organizations.

How to Join

Every spring and fall there are SGA senate elections. Get a petition form the student activites office and have 30 TAMUG students sign it. Once you return it and start campaigning!
